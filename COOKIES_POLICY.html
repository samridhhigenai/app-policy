Cookies Policy

1. Introduction

This Cookies Policy ("Policy") explains how Cash Manager App ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") uses cookies, similar tracking technologies, and local storage mechanisms when you use our mobile application ("Application").

2. What Are Cookies and Similar Technologies?

2.1 Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that contain information about your interaction with the Application. While traditional HTTP cookies are primarily used in web browsers, mobile applications use similar technologies for local data storage.

2.2 Similar Technologies

We also use:

  • Local Storage: Data stored locally on your device
  • Shared Preferences: Android's key-value storage system
  • UserDefaults: iOS's default values database
  • Analytics Trackers: Identifiers used for analytics purposes
  • Advertising IDs: Device identifiers for personalized advertising

3. Why We Use Cookies and Tracking Technologies

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Application to function properly:

  • Session Management: Keeping you logged in during your session
  • User Preferences: Remembering your app settings and language preferences
  • Security: Protecting against fraud and security threats
  • Performance: Optimizing app loading and functionality

Management: You cannot disable essential cookies as they are required for core functionality. However, you can clear them by uninstalling the Application or clearing app data.

3.2 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how you use the Application:

  • Usage Analytics: Tracking which features you use and how often
  • Crash Reporting: Identifying bugs and performance issues
  • User Flow: Understanding the path users take through the Application
  • Performance Metrics: Measuring app speed and responsiveness

Provider: Firebase Analytics (Google)
Management: You can disable analytics in app settings under "Privacy Settings"

3.3 Advertising Cookies

These cookies enable personalized advertising:

  • Ad Personalization: Serving ads relevant to your interests
  • Ad Performance: Measuring ad effectiveness and clicks
  • Ad Frequency: Preventing ad fatigue by limiting exposure
  • Conversion Tracking: Measuring if ads lead to app interactions

Provider: Google Mobile Ads
Management: You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings or app privacy settings

3.4 Functional Cookies

These enhance user experience:

  • Theme Preferences: Remembering dark/light mode selection
  • Language Settings: Retaining your language choice
  • UI Preferences: Storing layout and display preferences
  • Recently Used Data: Caching frequently accessed information

Management: Clear these by resetting to default settings in the Application

4. Cookies Used by Third-Party Services

4.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Analytics, performance monitoring, crash reporting, remote configuration
  • Data Collected: App events, user properties, crash data
  • Retention: Up to 12 months
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics in app settings
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Cookie Control: https://myactivity.google.com

4.2 Google Mobile Ads

  • Purpose: Serving and personalizing advertisements
  • Data Collected: Advertising ID, app interactions, ad impressions
  • Retention: As per Google's policies
  • Opt-Out:
    • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Personalized Ads (turn off)
    • Android: Settings > Google > Manage your Google Account > Data & Privacy > Ad personalization (turn off)
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.3 Remote Config

  • Purpose: Controlling app features and configuration remotely
  • Data Collected: App version, user properties
  • Retention: Cached locally
  • Opt-Out: Cannot opt out (essential for app functionality)

5. Local Data Storage

5.1 Types of Local Data Stored

  • Cash Records: Your financial entries and transactions
  • User Settings: App preferences, themes, language
  • Session Data: Login tokens and session information
  • Cache Data: Images, API responses for faster loading

5.2 Location of Storage

  • Android: Stored in app's designated directories and SharedPreferences
  • iOS: Stored in app's Documents or Library directories
  • Cloud: Optional Firebase backups if enabled

5.3 How to Clear Local Data

  • iOS: Settings > General > iPhone Storage > Cash Manager App > Offload App (keeps data) or Delete App (removes all data)
  • Android: Settings > Apps > Cash Manager App > Storage > Clear Cache or Clear Storage

6. Tracking and Device Identifiers

6.1 Advertising Identifier

  • What: A unique identifier assigned by your device's operating system
  • Purpose: For personalized advertising and analytics
  • Management: You can reset it in device settings
    • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Reset Advertising ID
    • Android: Settings > Google > Manage your Google Account > Data & Privacy > Ad ID > Reset advertising ID

6.2 Analytics Identifier

  • What: A unique identifier generated by Firebase for analytics
  • Purpose: Tracking app usage patterns
  • Retention: Persists until you clear app data

7. Your Cookie Choices and Rights

7.1 Cookie Management

You have several options:

In-App Settings:

  • Go to Settings or Privacy section
  • Toggle "Analytics" to enable/disable analytics tracking
  • Toggle "Personalized Ads" to enable/disable targeted advertising
  • Toggle "Performance Monitoring" to enable/disable crash reporting

Device Settings:

  • Disable personalized advertising in device settings
  • Reset your advertising ID
  • Limit ad tracking (iOS)
  • Opt out of analytics in device settings

Clear App Data:

  • Uninstall and reinstall the Application
  • Use device storage management to clear app cache/data
  • This removes all locally stored cookies and data

7.2 Do Not Track (DNT)

If you enable "Do Not Track" in your browser or device settings:

  • Third-party services may still collect limited data for app functionality
  • We will honor DNT preferences where technically feasible
  • Essential services cannot be disabled
