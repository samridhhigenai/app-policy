Welcome to Cash Manager App ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our application. We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To provide and maintain the app's features and services

4.2 Cloud Storage

  • Data may be backed up to Firebase (Google's cloud service) if you enable cloud backup

4.3 Security Measures
