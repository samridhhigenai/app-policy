Thank you for using Cash Manager App. We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your data from our app.

Important: Cash Manager App primarily stores your data locally on your device. Most of your data can be deleted directly from your device without contacting us. However, if you have enabled cloud backup features or want to ensure complete deletion, please follow the instructions below.

What Data is Deleted?

When you request data deletion, the following types of data will be removed:

  • Cash Records: All your financial entries, transactions, and balances
  • User Preferences: App settings, theme preferences, language settings
  • Cloud Backups: Any data backed up to Firebase (if cloud backup was enabled)
  • Analytics Data: Your usage patterns and app interaction data stored in Firebase Analytics
  • Crash Reports: Any crash logs associated with your device

What Data is Retained?

The following data may be retained for legal and operational purposes:

  • Anonymous Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify you personally (retained for up to 12 months)
  • Legal Records: Data required by law to be retained for compliance purposes
  • Advertising IDs: These are managed by Google and your device; reset them separately in device settings
